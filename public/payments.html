UPI allows instant bank-to-bank transfers via mobile apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM.

Steps to send money:

  1. Open your UPI app and authenticate.

  • Enable notifications for each transaction for instant alerts.

3. Digital Wallets

Wallets store money digitally for quick and secure payments. Examples include Paytm, Amazon Pay, and Mobikwik.

How to use:

  1. Add money using a bank account, card, or UPI.
  2. Pay merchants by scanning QR codes or entering phone number.
  3. Track all transactions within the app for easy budgeting.

Advanced tips:

  • Set transaction limits to prevent misuse
  • Use wallet apps from trusted sources only
  • Keep wallet PIN secure and private

4. Online Banking

Online banking allows you to manage accounts, pay bills, and transfer funds conveniently from home or mobile.

  • Use official bank apps or websites for security
  • Check balance, transfer funds, and pay bills easily
  • Enable two-factor authentication and OTP verification
  • Regularly monitor account statements to detect fraud

5. Safety Tips for Digital Payments

Keep your digital transactions secure with these tips:

  • Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords
  • Verify payment details before confirming transactions
  • Use only official apps and trusted websites
  • Keep devices secure with lock screens or biometrics
  • Report unauthorized transactions immediately
