Online forms allow you to apply for services or submit requests. Follow these steps to avoid errors:

5. Safety Tips for Online Government Services

6. DigiLocker: Store Your Documents Safely

DigiLocker is a secure cloud-based platform by the Government of India, allowing you to store and access documents digitally.

  • Store Aadhaar, PAN, driving license, educational certificates, insurance policies
  • Access official certificates issued directly by government agencies
  • Share verifiable documents digitally with schools, banks, or government offices
  • Secure, authentic, and accessible anytime from your phone or computer
  • Free service provided by the Government of India

Steps to upload:

  1. Visit DigiLocker and sign in using your mobile number and Aadhaar OTP.
  2. Click Upload Documents to add scanned copies.
  3. Organize documents into folders for easy access.
  4. Use Share to send verifiable copies.
